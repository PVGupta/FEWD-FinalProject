I am for the belief that we are all made up of parts of other people, and other people are made up of parts of us. Curious about the world and hungry for new experiences, I am inspired by everyday art, design, film, photography and conversations. Through my degree in Communication Studies and Art History from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), I learnt the importance of being present, and I try to implement this lesson into work I create, curate and partake in. Born in New Delhi, raised and currently living in Singapore, I am interested in the creation of meaning, and the symbiosis of image and text.
